The Origin

Founder's Story

As a biotechnology graduate, I have always been curious about what goes into the products we use every day.

During my journey of learning about ingredients and formulation science, I found myself asking a simple question: "Do we truly know what we apply to our skin every single day?"

In a world driven by speed and convenience, we’ve become surrounded by endless choices, long ingredient lists, and products that often leave consumers with more questions than answers.

It made me reflect on how different life was for our grandparents, whose daily routines were closely connected to nature and simple, familiar ingredients.

That reflection became the seed for Verra by Mugai.

I wanted to create a brand inspired by the wisdom of nature while respecting the principles of modern formulation science.

A brand where every ingredient is selected thoughtfully and every product is developed with care rather than compromise.

Verra by Mugai is not built on fear. It is built on awareness, curiosity, and conscious choices.

Because for us, skincare is more than a product.

The Name

The Meaning Behind Verra

Rooted in Every Beginning

Everything beautiful begins with strong roots. So does healthy skin.

The name Verra is inspired by the Tamil word "Ver" (வேர்), meaning root.

Roots are where nourishment begins. They provide strength, stability, and life.

At Verra by Mugai, we believe skincare should do the same—support your skin from its foundation rather than temporary solutions.
