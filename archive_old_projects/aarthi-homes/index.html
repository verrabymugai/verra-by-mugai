Welcome to Aarthi Homes

Escape to Kodaikanal's Pristine Hillside

Perched gracefully on the slopes of Attuvampatti, Aarthi Homes presents a gorgeous fusion of contemporary architecture and rustic warmth.

Our boutique villa stands out with its red gabled roof, charcoal facades, and terracotta accents. Surrounded by green valleys and the signature terraced step-farming of Kodaikanal, we offer a tranquil, fog-kissed haven away from the town center's hustle, yet keep you close to iconic attractions.

Kodaikanal Experiences

Moments Crafted to Last

Make your stay at Aarthi Homes unforgettable with unique experiences celebrating Kodaikanal's fresh atmosphere.

Authentic Home Dining

Indulge in steaming, freshly prepared local delicacies. From soft idlis and crisp dosas to warm hill-station lunches, our dishes are served hot and prepared with a touch of home.

Campfire & Star-Gazing

When the cold mountain air rolls down the valley, gather around our custom campfire zone. Enjoy the crackling fire, hot beverages, and watch the starry night sky over the pine trees.

Terraced Step-Farm Walks

Stroll through the scenic green potato and carrot step-farms that envelop our property. The quiet, winding roads of Attuvampatti make for a perfect morning walk in the fresh mist.

Our Location

Nestled in Serene Attuvampatti

Aarthi Homes is located in Attuvampatti, a quiet hillside enclave just a short drive from Kodaikanal's main lake.

Enjoying an elevated position, the villa overlooks gorgeous rolling mist-covered ridges and terraced step-farming plots. Our peaceful location ensures pure mountain air and absolute quiet, while remaining within close proximity to major scenic points.

Distance to Attractions:

  • Chettiar Park: 12 mins drive
  • Kodaikanal Lake & Town Center: 15 mins drive
  • Pepper Falls: 15 mins drive
  • Silver Cascade Falls: 18 mins drive
  • Coaker's Walk & Bryant Park: 20 mins drive
