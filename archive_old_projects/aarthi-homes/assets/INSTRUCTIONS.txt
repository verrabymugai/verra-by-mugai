AARTHI HOMES KODAIKANAL - MEDIA ASSETS DIRECTORY
==========================================================================

Please save your original photos and videos directly into this directory (d:\antygravity IDE\assets\) using the exact filenames listed below. 

The website's code and image lightbox are pre-configured to load these files:

1. "balcony.jpg"
   -> The balcony view overlooking the valley (with the tan chairs and square table).

2. "bedroom.jpg"
   -> The main wide bedroom photo showing the bed (with swan towels, logo-embroidered pillows, and the palm leaf mural).

3. "bedroom_ceiling.jpg"
   -> The bedroom ceiling shot showcasing the recessed LED line lights.

4. "nook.jpg"
   -> The cozy indoor nook containing the two armchairs and small table.

5. "corridor.jpg"
   -> The corridor/verandah walk with the red terracotta geometric screens.

6. "exterior.jpg"
   -> The close-up drone shot showing the building facade and the white SUV.

7. "aerial_valley.jpg"
   -> The wide drone shot showing the step farming terraces and mountain valley.

8. "video.mp4"
   -> The video tour or clip of the stay (this will play in the background of the hero section).

Note on Image Formats:
---------------------
If your images have a ".jpeg" or ".png" extension, you can either rename their extensions to ".jpg" to match the code, or edit the filenames in `index.html` and `app.js` to match your extensions. For the best loading performance, we recommend compressing the images before saving.
